# Concept

The second and final debate of the 2024 US Presidential Election takes place on September 10, 2024, at 9:00 p.m. EDT at the National Constitution Center in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Millions are watching. Two candidates. One moderator.

You, the moderator, have full control of the direction of tonight's debate. 

Every question you ask, every challenge you make, will shape the candidates' performance, their public image, and perhaps the fate of the nation for the next four years.
